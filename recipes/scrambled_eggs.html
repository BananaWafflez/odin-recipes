Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Optional: Milk

Steps

  1. Add eggs into a bowl, mix them up
  2. Add in the salt and pepper into the bowl
  3. Heat up pan to mid heat, add in butter
  4. Once butter has melted, spread it out in the pan
  5. Add in the eggs, lightly mix on the pan
  6. Once it looks halfway done, turn off the pan. Allow the residual heat to cook the rest of the eggs
  7. IMPORTANT: DO NOT OVER MIX THE EGGS!!
