Takoyaki

Takoyaki is a beloved dish in Japan. It consists of bonito flakes with a solid flour base with octopus in the middle, topped with some a light amount of mayo.

Ingredients

  • Dashi-flavored batter
  • Octopus
  • Pickled red ginger
  • Green Onion
  • Tempura Flakes
  • Takoyaki sauce

Steps

  1. Mix the batter
  2. Cut up the octopus, slice the green onions, have everything ready on the pan to flip the balls
  3. Oil the pan with a brush
  4. Whisk the batter, and pour into each individual hole in the pan.
  5. Add in the fillings
  6. Use the skewers to flip the takoyaki 90 degrees
  7. Add in the sauce once every single one is done!
